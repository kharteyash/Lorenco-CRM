====================================================================
 LeadNest — Feature Overview
 A realtor CRM. Every account manages its own leads, contacts, calls,
 appointments, follow-ups, and past clients. All data is private to the
 signed-in account. (Name is a placeholder.)
====================================================================


ACCOUNT & ACCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- Sign up / log in    Email + password. Passwords are hashed (bcrypt);
                      sessions are server-side cookies.
- Dark mode           Light/dark toggle in the top bar (moon/sun icon).
                      Remembers your choice; defaults to your device setting.
- Change password     Under Settings.


SECTIONS (sidebar)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

HOME  (dashboard)
- Stat row: active leads, appointments today, follow-ups due, past clients.
- Today's schedule: the day's appointments at a glance.
- Pipeline snapshot: a mini bar showing how many leads sit in each stage.
- Who to call: the top of your call queue.
- Due today: follow-up tasks due or overdue (tick them off here).
- Recent activity: a feed of your latest calls, new leads, and closings.

PIPELINE  (kanban board)
- Columns by stage: New -> Contacted -> Showing -> Offer -> Under Contract.
- Drag a lead card between columns to move it through the deal.
- Each card shows readiness, quick call/text/email actions, and budget.
- Click a card to open the full lead.

LEADS
- Your book of business: add, edit, search, and delete leads.
- Rich fields: contact info, intent (buying/selling/both), timeline,
  budget, property type, area/zip, financing, credit, assets, notes,
  stage, and source.
- Readiness score: each lead is auto-ranked High/Medium/Low from timeline,
  financing, credit, intent, and assets (drives the call queue).
- Lead drawer (click a lead): full details, an activity timeline
  (notes + logged calls), add notes, log a call, email them, edit,
  close as won, or delete.
- Log a call: record outcome (Connected/Voicemail/No Answer/Missed) + notes.
- Email a lead: send a 1:1 email from a template (Intro, Check-in, New
  listings, Thank you) via your Gmail; it's logged to the timeline.
- Close as won: capture the deal (type, price, address, date) and move the
  lead into Past Clients.
- Import CSV: bulk-add leads from a spreadsheet (auto-maps common columns).
- Intake form: a shareable public link (see LEAD CAPTURE below).

PAST CLIENTS
- Your closed deals and relationships to nurture.
- Add directly or via "Close as won"; import from CSV.
- Tracks deal type (Bought/Sold/Both), price, address, and close date.
- Quick call/text/email actions; edit or delete.

CONTACTS
- A simple address book for lenders, inspectors, title, vendors, etc.
- Name, company, phone, email, and a tag. Quick call/text/email actions.

CALLS
- Call queue: "who to call next," ranked by lead readiness. Skips anyone
  you've already called in the last 2 days.
- Log a call straight from the queue.
- Call history: a running log of every call and its outcome.

CALENDAR
- Month, Week, and Day views (Google Calendar style) of your appointments:
  Showings, Open Houses, Closings, Calls, Meetings, Other (color-coded).
- Follow-up tasks appear on their due date as all-day items; click one to
  jump to Follow-ups.
- Google Calendar sync (two-way): connect your Google account and every
  appointment you add / edit / delete here is mirrored into your Google
  Calendar; events from your Google Calendar show on the in-app calendar
  (read-only), including one-click Google Meet "Join" links.
- Click a day (or an hour slot in Week/Day view) to add; click an
  appointment to edit. Prev/next/today nav; "+N more" opens that day.
- A "now" line marks the current time in Week/Day view.
- Appointments can link to a lead and carry a location + notes.
- "Upcoming" list below the month grid.

FOLLOW-UPS  (tasks)
- Your task list with due dates and priority (High/Medium/Low).
- Add, complete, reopen, edit, delete. Overdue items are flagged.
- Automatic follow-ups (optional, on by default): the app creates reminders
  for you --
    * Call every new lead (that has a phone).
    * Retry after a missed call / voicemail.
    * Nudge when a hot lead goes quiet.
    * Check in on urgent-timeline leads.
  Toggle this off in Settings.

REPORTS
- This month: new leads, deals closed, and closed volume.
- Lead -> close conversion rate.
- Pipeline funnel: a proportional view across every stage plus Closed.
- Lead-source ROI: leads vs. closed deals per source, with conversion %,
  so you can see which channels actually produce business.

AUTO EMAILS  (weekly mailing list)
- Build a mailing list (add addresses one at a time or paste several).
- Write one email template (subject + message) that you can edit anytime.
- Schedule a weekly send (pick the weekday, toggle on/off).
- "Send to everyone now" for an immediate blast.
- Connect Gmail: send as yourself via your own Google account. Falls back
  to shared SMTP if configured. Recent sends are logged.

SETTINGS
- Automatic follow-ups on/off.
- Change password.


CROSS-CUTTING FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- LEAD CAPTURE (intake form): each account gets a public /apply/<link>.
  Share it on your site, social bio, or email signature; submissions drop
  straight into your Leads, tagged source "Intake form." Includes spam
  protection; you can reset the link anytime.
- READINESS SCORING: a single scoring model powers the lead badges, the
  call queue, and the "who to call" list on Home.
- ACTIVITY TIMELINE: notes, logged calls, and sent emails all appear on a
  lead's timeline in one place.
- AI FRESHNESS: the day after each weekly send (send Monday → new draft
  Tuesday), AI writes next week's edition in the same voice with a fresh
  angle, so subscribers never get a repeat. Also on demand via the
  "Write next week's with AI" button. Models are tried in a fallback
  chain (Gemini models first, then llm7.io's other chat models) until
  one answers; set GEMINI_API_KEY / LLM7_API_KEY to enable.
- CONNECT GOOGLE: connect your Google account once; the weekly email and
  1:1 lead emails send from your own address via the Gmail API, and the
  calendar syncs two-way with your Google Calendar. (Accounts connected
  before calendar sync existed just reconnect once to grant the extra
  calendar permission.)


TECH (for reference)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- Backend:  Node + Express, PostgreSQL, bcrypt, cookie sessions.
- Frontend: static single-page app (HTML + vanilla JS + Tailwind/Lucide
            via CDN). No build step.
- Email:    Gmail API (per-user OAuth) or shared SMTP; weekly dispatcher
            via an internal timer + an external cron endpoint.
- Deploy:   any Node host with a DATABASE_URL (Render blueprint included).
            Tables are created automatically on first start.
